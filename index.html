Local Data Insights (RO–MD)

Local Data Insights (LDI) is an independent data project focused on local markets and business ecosystems in Romania and Moldova.

We publish structured datasets, spatial visualizations, and market snapshots to support analytical, entrepreneurial, and research use cases.

What we provide

  • City-level datasets of local businesses
  • Normalized categories and verified locations
  • Geographic coordinates and density views
  • Market snapshots for selected industries
