Local Data Insights (Romania–Moldova)

Local Data Insights (LDI) develops structured, analysis-ready datasets about local business ecosystems.

Publicly available digital information is transformed into normalized, verified, and spatially structured market snapshots ready for research, strategic planning, and analytical use.

What we provide

  • City-level datasets of local businesses
  • Verified and normalized business entities
  • Geographic coordinates and direct map references
  • Structured market snapshots for selected industries
  • Data ready for Excel, BI tools, and GIS systems

Who this is for

  • Market researchers
  • Industry operators and healthcare providers
  • Investors and suppliers
  • Consultants and analysts
  • Academic researchers
